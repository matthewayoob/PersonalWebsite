Beyond the screen
A little about me

I'm a Stanford graduate with a deep belief that the most meaningful technology is the kind that brings people in, not leaves them out. Three degrees later — a BS in CS:HCI, a BA in Music, and an MS in CS:AI — I've learned that the best products, like the best performances, are felt before they're understood.

My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, product thinking, and human behavior. Whether designing experiments for Instagram's ranking systems or prototyping with frontier models, I'm always looking for the human angle.

Outside of work I make visual art, perform music, and think a lot about accessibility — the conviction that technology should expand possibility for everyone, not just the people it was built for.
